My Specialties

I am an energetic and motivated web designer. Focusing on front end design, I bring dreams to life.

Drupal 7

Drupal is one of the world's most popular CMS platforms. It is highly customizable, and both a seasoned web developer and a casual blogger can feel at home using it. I began my web design career in Drupal, and I fell in love with it at once.

HTML 5

I started using HTML right after I started my first contract with the University of Northern Iowa, in order to add menu functionality to a website. Needless to say, HTML is an essential tool for any developer.

CSS3

Introduced to CSS right off the bat in my Drupal course, I quickly became fascinated by this tool. My final project in the Drupal class had my professor's head in the header. Spinning. It's gone downhill ever since then!

Graphics

I began using Photoshop in high school, when Photoshop CS2 first came out. I love to put together an idea formed in my head on a blank canvas. Web designers need to have a wide range of abilities, and knowing Photoshop is an invaluable skill.
